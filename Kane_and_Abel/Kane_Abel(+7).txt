The story revolves around two men who by some strange fate are born on the same day in 2 different parts of the world.....but destiny makes them each other's worst enemies. One a polish immigrant and the other the son of a Bostonian millionaire heir of a large fortune.. Read about their life struggle as both these ambitious men try to outwit each other.. A tale of ambition, power, struggle, lust blends all aspects to be the bestselling best seller ever. The climax is sure to melt your hearts away it was simply unbelievable. If anyone can guess the ending without reading it i'd be willing to give a million bucks to him. A must read novel.
